# Week 2026-W26 (Jun 22–28) — Base Phase, Week 2

## Context

**FTP test week.** Saturday's long ride is replaced with a structured test protocol to establish your actual power zones. All bike training from next week onwards will be zone-based on real numbers rather than estimates.

Tuesday morning introduces short structured run intervals — keep them controlled, this is base phase not race prep.

**Zones reference** (HR-based until Saturday's FTP test):
- Z2 (aerobic): 125–154 bpm | Run ~4:48–5:34/km
- Z3 (tempo): 155–169 bpm | Run ~4:18–4:48/km
- Z4 (threshold): 170–184 bpm | Run ~4:02–4:18/km

### Sat Jun 27
**FTP Test Protocol** — ~75min total

This replaces the normal long ride. Follow this protocol exactly:

1. **Warm-up** — 15min easy Z2 spinning (125–154 bpm)
2. **2 × 1min high-cadence efforts** — 100+ rpm, easy gear, 2min recovery between
3. **5min easy recovery**
4. **20-minute all-out effort** — ride as hard as you can sustain for the full 20 minutes. Consistent effort from the start — don't sprint and blow up. Target: highest possible average power/HR you can hold. Flat or very consistent route (avoid traffic lights).
5. **Cool-down** — 15min easy spin

**How to calculate FTP**: Take your average power from the 20-minute effort × 0.95. Update `STATUS.md` with the result and update `plans/macro.md` Milestones.

_If you don't have a power meter: note your average HR during the 20min effort — this becomes your threshold HR reference._
