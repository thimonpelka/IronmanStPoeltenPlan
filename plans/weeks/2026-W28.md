**Morning Run — Tempo** (~55min)

Warm up 15min easy (Z1–Z2). Then: **2 × 10min @ Z3 tempo** (155–169 bpm, ~4:18–4:48/km),
3min easy jog between. Then **4 × 20s strides** (relaxed and fast, full recovery). Cool

not 5k speed (see macro *Run & Brick Strategy*). Strides keep leg speed/economy without
the VO2 cost. Keep the tempo controlled — conversational-plus, not a race.
