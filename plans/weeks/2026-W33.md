# Week 2026-W33 (Aug 10–16) — Base Phase, Week 9

## Context

Back to a **load week** after the W32 deload, and the start of the late-base ramp. Two
progressions:

1. **First open-water swim** (Fri) — the OW season is warm now; start the open-water
   skills block (macro: OW block 1, Aug–Oct). Comfort and sighting over speed.
2. **First sweet-spot bike** (Thu) — nudging the mid-week ride from tempo toward
   sweet-spot now that power zones are established.

Swim stays 3×/week (Tue pool / Wed pool / Fri open water).

**Zones** (FTP 238W): Bike by power. Run by HR + pace.
- Bike Z2 = 131–178W (56–75%) · Sweet-spot = 209–224W (88–94%)
- Run Z2 = 125–154 bpm · Z3 tempo = 155–169 bpm

---

## Schedule

### Mon Aug 10
**06:30 — Gym — Lower body** (~60min)

Back to full strength block after the deload: squat 4×5–6, RDL, split squat, calf, core.
Aim to progress load vs W31. See `plans/strength.md`.

---

### Tue Aug 11
**Morning Run — Tempo** (~60min)

Warm up 15min. Then: **2 × 15min @ Z3 tempo**, 3min easy jog between. **4 × 20s strides**.
Cool down 10min. Longer sustained tempo than pre-deload — this is the durability work.

**Afternoon — Swim (pool)** (~50min) — technique + continuous. ~1200–1400m.

---

### Wed Aug 12
**06:30 — Gym — Upper + core** (~60min)

Push/pull/overhead + core circuit. Low leg load so Thu's sweet-spot ride is fresh. See
`plans/strength.md`.

**Evening — Swim (pool)** (~45min) — drills + rhythm. ~1100m.

---

### Thu Aug 13
**Mid-Week Ride — Sweet-Spot** (~90min)

Warm up 15min Z2 (131–178W). Then: **2 × 15min @ sweet-spot (209–224W)**, 6min easy
between. Cool down 15min. First sweet-spot session — it should feel "comfortably hard,"
sustainable, not threshold. Back off if power drifts down in the second interval.

---

### Fri Aug 14
**First Open Water Swim** (~30–40min)

Warm, calm water; ideally with someone present. Goals, in order: **be comfortable**,
breathe calmly without a wall to grab, **practise sighting** (look forward every 6–8
strokes), swim in short straight-line segments. Distance is irrelevant. Get out if cold
or uneasy — build exposure gradually.

---

### Sat Aug 15
**Long Ride** — Z2 endurance, ~3h, ~85–95km, **real elevation** (aim 600–900m).

Firmly Z2 (**131–178W**, ~150–170W on the flats; let watts climb naturally on the hills).
This is the aerobic + climbing anchor for the race's hilly bike course. Fuel 60–80g
carbs/h from 45min.

---

### Sun Aug 16
**Long Run** — Z2, ~20km, ~1:50h

Consistent Z2 (125–154 bpm). Optional late-run progression: if it feels easy past 16km,
lift the final 3km to steady Z3 — only if HR has been stable.
