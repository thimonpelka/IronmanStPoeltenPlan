# Week 2026-W30 (Jul 20–26) — Base Phase, Week 6

## Context

**First week of independent swim training.** Two swim sessions per week from here on: Tuesday afternoon and Friday. Sessions start short and technique-focused — apply what the course taught. Distance is secondary to quality of movement.

Apply technique from the course. Keep the session short and drill-heavy. Suggested structure:

Total ~900m. Stop if form deteriorates. Distance is irrelevant at this stage.
