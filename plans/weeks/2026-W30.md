### Tue Jul 21
**Morning Run — Tempo** (~55min)

Warm up 15min easy. Then: **2 × 12min @ Z3 tempo** (155–169 bpm, ~4:18–4:48/km), 3min

**Afternoon — First Independent Swim** (~45min)

Apply technique from the course. Keep the session short and drill-heavy. Suggested structure:
- 200m easy warm-up
- 4 × 50m catch-up drill (rest 20s)
- 4 × 50m fingertip drag drill (rest 20s)
- 4 × 100m focus stroke (rest 30s) — concentrate on one technical cue at a time
- 100m easy cool-down

Total ~900m. Stop if form deteriorates. Distance is irrelevant at this stage.
