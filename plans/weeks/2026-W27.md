### Sat Jul 4
**FTP Test Protocol** — ~75min total

Replaces long ride. Follow this protocol exactly:

1. **Warm-up** — 15min easy Z2 spinning (125–154 bpm)
2. **2 × 1min high-cadence efforts** — 100+ rpm, easy gear, 2min recovery between
3. **5min easy recovery**
4. **20-minute all-out effort** — ride as hard as you can sustain for the full 20 minutes. Consistent effort from the start. Target: highest possible average power/HR you can hold. Flat or very consistent route (avoid traffic lights).
5. **Cool-down** — 15min easy spin

**How to calculate FTP**: average power from the 20min effort × 0.95. Record in `STATUS.md` and update `plans/macro.md`. If no power meter: note average HR during the 20min effort as threshold HR reference.
