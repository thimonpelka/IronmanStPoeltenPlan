# Weekly Schedule Template

## Constraints

- **No outdoor bike sessions before work on weekdays.** An indoor smart trainer arrives ~end of Oct 2026 (ADR 0004); from then, weekday trainer rides are allowed but preferred **after work** — Mon/Wed mornings stay gym, Tue morning stays run.
- **Morning sessions on work days** are limited to: Strength, easy run, or short run (≤1h).
- Swim sessions may be added to morning slots once a pool routine is established (TBD).
